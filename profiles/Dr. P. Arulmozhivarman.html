Prof. Dr. P. Arulmozhivarman

Vice Chancellor

VIT-AP University, India

Biography

Prof. Dr. P. Arulmozhivarman is a visionary leader, professor, and researcher currently serving as the Vice Chancellor of VIT-AP University, India. He holds a Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology, Trichy, and has over a decade of academic leadership experience.

Professional Contributions

He previously served as Dean of Academic Research and Dean of Electrical Engineering at VIT, Vellore. He bridges academia with industry giants (Robert Bosch, Samsung, GE) and fosters global collaborations with elite institutions like Johns Hopkins, MIT, Harvard, and Oxford.

Delivering training & research in:

  • AI Innovation & Healthcare Product Development
  • Biomedical Signal & Image Processing
  • Photonics Systems & Machine Vision
Research & Innovation

He has secured prestigious grants from DRDO, ISRO, and DST. His work includes over 95 publications in esteemed journals, several granted patents, and the mentorship of 14+ Ph.D. candidates.

Awards & Recognition

He is a Senior Member of the IEEE Signal Processing Society and serves as an expert reviewer for prestigious IEEE journals.
