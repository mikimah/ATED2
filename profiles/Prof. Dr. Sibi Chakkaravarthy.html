Prof. Dr. Sibi Chakkaravarthy

Director, Centre of Excellence

VIT-AP University, India

Biography

Prof. Dr. Sibi Chakkaravarthy is a Professor in the School of Computer Science & Engineering at VIT-AP University, India. He holds a Ph.D. in Network and System Security from Anna University and is the Founder & CEO of DigitalFortress Private Limited.

Professional Contributions

He serves as the Coordinator for the Centre of Excellence in AI and Robotics (AIR) at VIT-AP. He is an experienced speaker for NPTEL on Practical Cyber Security and possesses strong technical expertise in network security, malware analysis, and cloud deployment.

Delivering training & research in:

  • Network Security & Advanced Cyber Security
  • Incident Response Management & Digital Forensics
  • Targeted Cyber Attacks & Advanced Persistent Threats
Research & Innovation

He has secured major research grants from DST, DSCI, and NVIDIA. His innovative projects include India's first academic 3D-printed humanoid robot (VISU), smart garments, and chatbots. He has authored 40+ SCIE journal publications and holds multiple patents.

Awards & Recognition

He has received the Best Research Mentor Award from HPE, the Technology Innovation Award from IBM Systems, and a Global Speaker Grant from the University System of New Hampshire.
