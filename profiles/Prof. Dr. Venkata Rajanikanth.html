Prof. Dr. Venkata Rajanikanth Machavaram

Professor of Physics

VIT-AP University, India

Biography

Prof. Dr. Venkata Rajanikanth Machavaram is an experimental physicist and Professor of Physics at VIT-AP University, India. He holds a Ph.D. in Optical Fibre Sensors Engineering from Cranfield University, UK.

Professional Contributions

He spent 6 years as a Research Fellow at the University of Birmingham, UK, and served as Research & International Relations Coordinator at VIT. He has built advanced laboratories funded by international agencies like Rolls-Royce and EPSRC.

Delivering training & research in:

  • Optical Fibre Communications & Sensors
  • Optoelectronics & Applied Optics
  • Materials Science & Structural Health Monitoring
Research & Innovation

He holds 2 granted US patents, has co-supervised 8 successful Ph.D. projects, and authored numerous high-impact journal papers (h-index: 13).

Awards & Recognition

He won the Best Research Paper Award (ISSIP'16) and serves as an expert reviewer for prestigious journals like Optics Express and IEEE Photonics Journal.
