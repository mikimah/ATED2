Biography

Dr. Zulkifli Jalil is a cybersecurity educator and industry practitioner with over 30 years of IT experience, including more than 20 years specialising in cybersecurity.

He holds a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) from Teesside University (United Kingdom) and a Master of Science in Information Technology.
