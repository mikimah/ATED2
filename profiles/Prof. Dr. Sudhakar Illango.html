Prof. Dr. Sudhakar Illango

Dean, School of Computer Science and Engineering

VIT-AP University, India

Biography

Prof. Dr. Sudhakar Illango is an accomplished academician, researcher, and administrator with over 18 years of experience in higher education. He holds a Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Anna University and currently serves as the Dean of the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE) at VIT-AP University, India.

Professional Contributions

As Dean of SCOPE, he leads academic planning, research initiatives, and strategic growth. He previously served as the Programme Chair for the TCS-supported CSBS programme (2020-2023) and was the Convenor of VITopia, the university's flagship fest, for two consecutive years (2023 and 2024).

Delivering training & research in:

  • Image Processing & Computer Vision
  • Deep Learning & Medical Imaging
  • Agricultural AI, Remote Sensing, & IoT Security
Research & Innovation

He has published over 20 works in reputed international journals and conferences, accumulating 280+ citations. His notable research includes deep learning-based disease detection in agriculture and autism detection. He has successfully guided 1 Ph.D. scholar to completion and is supervising 2 others.

Awards & Recognition

His research has achieved significant global visibility, including a highly cited publication on Big Data clustering that received over 100 citations.
