CONFERENCE OVERVIEW

In the context of rapid advancements in Artificial Intelligence (AI), interdisciplinary collaboration and practical applications have become extremely important. The international conference with the theme “Shaping the Future with AI: Innovation, Integration and Impact” is organized by Saigon Technology University (STU) in collaboration with domestic and international partners. The event, titled the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Technology, Engineering and Design, is scheduled to take place from October 29 to October 31, 2026. The conference will gather scientists, experts, and practitioners to exchange knowledge, share experiences, and promote collaboration in AI, technology, engineering, and design. The organizers cordially invite scholars, experts, and enterprises to submit papers and participate in shaping the future with AI for sustainable development and societal value.

Sai Gon Technology University (STU), 180 Cao Lo street, Chanh Hung Ward, Ho Chi Minh City, VietNam
