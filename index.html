CONFERENCE OVERVIEW

In the context of rapid advancements in Artificial Intelligence (AI), which has become a core driver of global innovation, the connection of knowledge, enhancement of interdisciplinary collaboration, and promotion of AI applications in practice are increasingly essential. Under the theme “Shaping the Future with AI: Innovation, Integration and Impact” , the conference aims to establish an academic and practical forum for sharing cutting-edge research, fostering technology transfer, and generating positive societal impacts. Saigon Technology University (STU), in collaboration with domestic and international partners, is pleased to organize the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Technology, Engineering and Design, scheduled to take place from October 29 to October 31, 2026. The conference will bring together scientists, experts, and practitioners to exchange academic knowledge, share practical experiences, and promote collaboration in the fields of artificial intelligence, technology, engineering, and design. We cordially invite scholars, experts, and enterprises to submit papers and participate in the conference, contributing to shaping the future with AI towards sustainable development and meaningful societal values.

ABOUT HO CHI MINH CITY

Ho Chi Minh City, formerly known as Saigon, is the largest city and the economic, cultural, and technological center of Vietnam. Home to more than nine million residents, the city is renowned for its vibrant energy, diverse culture, and unique blend of historical heritage and modern development.

Visitors to Ho Chi Minh City can explore a variety of iconic landmarks that reflect the city's rich history and rapid transformation. Among the most recognizable is Landmark 81, the tallest building in Vietnam and one of the tallest skyscrapers in Southeast Asia, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the city. Another famous symbol is the Bitexco Financial Tower, whose distinctive design was inspired by Vietnam's national flower, the lotus. The tower features the Saigon Skydeck, a popular observation deck overlooking the city skyline.

Other notable attractions include Ben Thanh Market, one of the oldest and most famous markets in Vietnam; the Independence Palace, a historic site associated with significant events in Vietnamese history; Notre-Dame Cathedral of Saigon, a remarkable example of French colonial architecture; and Nguyen Hue Walking Street, a lively pedestrian boulevard surrounded by modern buildings, cafés, restaurants, and entertainment venues.

In addition to its landmarks, Ho Chi Minh City is celebrated for its world-famous cuisine, welcoming atmosphere, and dynamic urban lifestyle. The city offers visitors an unforgettable experience where tradition and modernity coexist, making it one of the most attractive destinations in Southeast Asia for business travelers, tourists, and international conference participants.
